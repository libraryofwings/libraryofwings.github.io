Library of Wings

I'm an engineering tech and aviculturist with an interest in elevators and ornithology. This website serves as a creative outlet for my writings & drawings about life, the universe, and everything - but mostly birds.

Companions

Meet Hazel, a Harz Roller canary hatched in June 2024. Known for her vegetable thievery, she is brave and companionable and will chatter away on my desk all day, pausing to jump away and look out the window at passing traffic. Her favorite food is bell peppers but she will happily demolish anything green (including houseplants.)

Next is Juniper, a Lionhead rabbit born in October 2024. She is quite shy, but warms up quickly to cuddles and snacks. She likes to race around on rugs but refuses to step on hard floors. Though endlessly spoiled, she has a sweet personality and soft fur too. Her favorite food is red cabbage.
